So, where to start?

I'm not often told the details of our past projects - I came in after the outpost outside Justice was built. But from what I know, we're here to help. Trying to stabilize the area, physically and socially.

Things were pretty normal up until a few weeks ago. The activity outdoors ramped up as a wave of new arrivals came - seemingly from nowhere. That train hasn't worked in years.

It culminated in something unprecedented - an entity nearing the town's center. Forces were immediately dispatched with emergency weaponry - and that was it. The team is missing - presumed dead. The entity is gone. But most importantly, the sun hasn't risen since.

We keep noticing odd behavior. Initially, it was isolated to a few individuals and attributed to some sort of cabin fever. But it got worse, and we did our best to inform people. We'll see how well that works.

And now, we're getting readings from far-off areas. It seems that these events aren't just happening here.

The administration isn't doing anything. I haven't heard anything from headquarters since we found those signals. It's just us.
